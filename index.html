Why Choose Our Online Examination System?

Designed specifically for Ethio-Italy Polytechnic College with your needs in mind

Secure & Fair

Role-based access, encrypted data storage, randomized questions, and timed exams to ensure integrity.

Instant Results

Automated evaluation of objective questions with immediate feedback and detailed performance analytics.

Access Anywhere

Take exams from any device on campus network. No need to travel to examination centers.

Time Efficient

Reduce exam preparation time by 80% and eliminate manual grading that takes 10-20 days per semester.

Cost Effective

Save 45,000-60,000 Birr on printing and 25,000-30,000 Birr on printer cartridges annually.

Detailed Reports

Comprehensive performance analytics for instructors and administrators.

About Us

Ethio-Italy Polytechnic College

Excellence in Technology Education Since 2010 E.C.

Modernizing Education Through Technology

Ethio-Italy Polytechnic College, located in Dire Dawa, Ethiopia, has been at the forefront of technical and vocational education. The Department of Information Technology is committed to providing students with cutting-edge tools and platforms for academic excellence.

The Online Examination System is a student-led project developed by the 2026 graduating class to transform traditional paper-based examinations into a secure, efficient, and accessible digital platform.
